    Product Quality Health Assessment

    Skip to end of metadata
    • Created by Peter Varga, last modified on Apr 19, 2021
    Go to start of metadata


    The Product Quality Health Assessment is dedicated to helping teams to assess their product's health, with respect to quality.

    This assessment helps teams to:

    • focus on quality concepts
    • gain visibility into tracking progress of quality improvements
    • provide guidance/processes for improving quality

    Teams are expected to complete one assessment per product.

    The main PHA Site - can be used for evaluating your team's product's health.

    This space can be accessed directly using http://shorty/qhealth OR http://shorty/phadocs

    NOTE: This content is aligned with Agile Transformation Delivery Team Maturity Model (A local copy of the helper spreadsheet form is also uploaded here: "Agile-Team-Self-Assessment" Form)

     

    Legend: Agile Transformation Maturity Model

    Agile Maturity Level
    Description

    LEVEL I

    		Team is just starting to consider quality concepts, and beginning to integrate basic quality processes/concepts

    LEVEL II

    		Team is actively implementing on quality concepts, and tracking progress/improvements

    LEVEL III

    Team is organized around products, focused on delivering quality applications, with a quality first mindset - starting to implement on more advanced quality concepts

    LEVEL IV

    Team is effective within their releases and sprints, and actively delivering high quality applications - having implemented many advanced quality concepts

    LEVEL V

    		Team and Product has all of the quality concepts implemented, consistently delivering high quality applications in an automated manner, measuring results/improvements, and have well-documented implementation/processes


    Progressive measure: If you are not doing everything in one of the maturity rating, then you will not be advancing further until you complete all of them.
